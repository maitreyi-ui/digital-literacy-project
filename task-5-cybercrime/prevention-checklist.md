✔ Stay Safe Online Checklist

Do not click on suspicious or unknown links

Always verify the sender’s email address

Use strong and unique passwords

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

Avoid sharing personal or login details online

Double-check URLs before entering sensitive information

Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone

Use trusted apps and websites for payments

Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in

Call helpline 1930 for immediate assistance
